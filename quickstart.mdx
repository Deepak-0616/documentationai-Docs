## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: The Kakarot mobile app from the App Store or Google Play

</Callout>

## Create a Free Trial Account

Kakarot offers a 7-day free trial to explore the full library. Follow these steps to sign up.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://app.kakarot.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user-plus">
    Provide your email, create a password, and select the free trial option. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Kakarot and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In and Set Up Your Profile

Once verified, log in to personalize your experience.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Login" icon="log-in">
    Go to `https://app.kakarot.com/login` and enter your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="settings">
    Add a profile picture, set your watch preferences, and choose content ratings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Dashboard" icon="home">
    You'll land on the dashboard showing new releases and popular simulcasts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browse Categories and Genres

Kakarot organizes content into genres like Action, Shonen, and Fantasy. Use the navigation menu to discover shows.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Click **Categories** in the top menu, then select **Genres**. Browse **Shonen** for high-energy anime.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Tap the bottom navigation bar for **Browse**. Swipe through **Popular** or search genres directly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Search and Start Streaming

Find and play your first episode quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use Search" icon="search">
    Type "Dragon Ball" in the search bar. Results show episodes, manga, and related content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Content" icon="play-circle">
    Choose an episode like **Dragon Ball Episode 1**. Click **Play** to start streaming in HD.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Settings" icon="sliders">
    Use player controls for subtitles, speed (`<1.5x`), and quality. Add to your Crunchylist for later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Streams adapt to your connection. For best quality, use Wi-Fi and enable auto-quality.
</Callout>
