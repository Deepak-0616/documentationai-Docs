---
title: Set Up a New Project in KAKAROT
description: Quick guide to your first project setup.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Open your account at [dashboard.kakarot.com](https://dashboard.kakarot.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click **Create Project**, enter a name, and optionally add a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Template">
    Select a starting template or blank project, then confirm your choices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Documenting">
    You can now add pages, sections, and manage your documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
