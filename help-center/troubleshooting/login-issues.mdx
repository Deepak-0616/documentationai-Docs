---
title: Can't Log In to KAKAROT
description: Steps to fix KAKAROT login problems.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page reloads or shows an error
- Password reset not working

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear cookies and cache, then try again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues persist, email support@kakarot.com.
  </Step>
</Steps>
